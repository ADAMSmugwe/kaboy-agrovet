About Us

Our Story

Kaboy Agrovet was established in 2010 with a mission to empower local farmers in Kianjai, Meru County with high-quality agricultural inputs and expert advice. What started as a small agrovet shop has grown into a trusted agricultural hub serving hundreds of farmers in the region.

Our team consists of experienced agronomists and veterinary professionals who are passionate about helping farmers achieve better yields and healthier livestock. We believe in sustainable farming practices that benefit both the farmer and the environment.

At Kaboy Agrovet, we don't just sell products - we build relationships and provide solutions tailored to your farming needs.
